THE PERSONS WHO INSPIRE US

DEAN BLUNT

Dean Blunt, is a British musician, record producer, singer-songwriter, writer, director, and artist. He's best known for both his work as part of avant-garde group Hype Williams (featuring himself and Inga Copeland) and more recently, the groups Babyfather and Blue Iverson.

QUENTIN TARANTINO

Quentin Jerome Tarantino born March 27, 1963 (age 57) in Knoxville, Tennessee, U.S. He is an American film director, screenwriter, producer, and actor.
