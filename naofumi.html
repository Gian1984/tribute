Naofumi Iwatani seen by my eyes

The Hero with the Shield is the protagonist of the story. Naofumi Iwatani was a sophomore at college: he was summoned while reading a book about four heroes individually holding a specific weapon. Although originally a socially awkward and kind person, his experiences with Myne Melromarc very soon after being summoned he remains dark and cynical: however, he remains a person full of generosity and empathy beneath his tunes. austere. Although he had a difficult start due to his unjust disgrace and the machinations of Princess Myne, and is therefore at a much lower level than the other heroes because by all means prevented from progressing as easily as 'them, he is always the one of the four who knows how to use his weapon the best and, thanks to his experience and his personal efforts, has the most ability and power. Intelligent in his self-learning, he understands how to best "feed" his shield and then possesses a wide variety of techniques: numerous shields which improve his statistics and those of his allies while reducing those of his opponents. His curse weapon is the Shield of Wrath, which gives him immense strength when in the grip of his most violent feelings (hatred, anger, revenge ...), however he can lose control of himself by Using him and his stats remain drastically lowered for a while. It also becomes the shields of the Seven New Deadly Sins, which represent the sins of modern man and carry more bizarre and abstract drawbacks. He was twenty years old and had been summoned, making him the second highest of the four summoned heroes.
